01. About Me

My journey into software engineering started with automating a simple math problem and grew into building scalable, real-world systems. At Clarivate Analytics, I spent three years shipping production-grade features, owning CI/CD, and contributing to reliable architectures. Along the way, I learned to balance speed with rigor—keeping delivery fast while safeguarding quality, accessibility, and resilience.

Now pursuing an MS in Software Engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington, I’m deepening system design, Spring Boot, and AWS cloud skills—aiming for clean, maintainable full-stack systems. I love pairing strong design with pragmatic delivery, collaborating closely with teams to keep performance, scalability, and long-term maintainability aligned. Outside work, I recharge with video games, hiking, and football.
